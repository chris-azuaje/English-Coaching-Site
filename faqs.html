Frequently asked questions

What age groups and levels do you teach?

I specialize in teaching students of all age groups, from young children to adults. My teaching covers a wide range of levels, from beginners who are just starting to learn English, to advanced learners looking to refine their fluency and grasp of complex grammatical structures. Each lesson is tailored to meet the specific needs and goals of the student.

How do you personalize your tutoring for each student?

I begin with an initial assessment to understand each student's current level of English, learning style, and objectives. Based on this assessment, I create a personalized learning plan that targets the student's specific areas for improvement, whether that's expanding vocabulary, mastering grammar, improving pronunciation, or enhancing conversational skills. I also incorporate interests and hobbies into lessons to make learning engaging and relevant.

What materials and methods do you use in your teaching?

I use a variety of materials and methods in my teaching, including textbooks, online resources, multimedia content, and practical exercises. I focus on a balanced approach that includes reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. Interactive activities, such as role-plays, discussions, and storytelling, are key components of my teaching method to ensure lessons are engaging and effective.

How do you track and assess progress?

Progress is assessed continuously through observation during lessons, regular quizzes, and assignments. I provide constructive feedback on all work done by the student and conduct formal assessments at regular intervals to track improvement and adjust the learning plan as needed. Students and parents (for younger learners) receive regular progress reports detailing achievements and areas for further development.

What is your cancellation policy?

I understand that unexpected events can occur, leading to the need to reschedule or cancel a lesson. I kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. For cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice, unless due to emergency situations, a cancellation fee may apply. This policy helps manage scheduling and ensures that I can offer the missed time slot to other students.
